01 OHM'S LAW
FIND VOLTAGE
V = I × R
Current × Resistance = Voltage
FIND CURRENT
I = V / R
Voltage ÷ Resistance = Current
FIND RESISTANCE
R = V / I
Voltage ÷ Current = Resistance

03 POWER FORMULA
⚡
P = V × I
Measured in Watts (W) — critical for motor & battery selection
P = V × I P = I² × R P = V² / R
🔋 12V motor drawing 2A = 24W
🔋 5V Arduino at 0.1A = 0.5W
🔋 6V servo at 0.5A = 3W
04 SERIES vs PARALLEL CIRCUITS
SERIES CIRCUIT
R_total = R1 + R2 + R3
Current is identical everywhere
Voltage splits across each resistor
One break = entire circuit fails
PARALLEL CIRCUIT
1/R_total = 1/R1 + 1/R2 + 1/R3
Voltage is identical everywhere
Current splits across branches
One break = others still work
